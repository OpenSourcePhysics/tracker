My Local Library

Your local library lets you collect Tracker videos and video experiments as holdings (local and remote files) organized into catalogs (folders). You can share your library with others by uploading it to a network or web server.

To manage your library, click the Edit button.

More information about Tracker libraries is available at <http://www.cabrillo.edu/~dbrown/tracker/>.

Online Libraries

The ComPADRE Pathway, a part of the National Science Digital Library, is a growing network of educational resource collections supporting teachers and students in Physics and Astronomy. As a user you may explore collections designed to meet your specific needs and help build the network by recommending resources, commenting on resources, and starting or joining discussions.

More information about the OSP comPADRE Collection is available at <http://www.compadre.org/osp/>. To recommend a Tracker experiment for the collection, visit the Suggest a Resource page at <http://www.compadre.org/osp/items/suggest.cfm>. Please contact the OSP Collection editor, Wolfgang Christian, for additional information about Tracker experiments in this collection.
