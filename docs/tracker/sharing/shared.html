Shared Collections

June 2024

These collections have been shared by Tracker users and content developers. Listed alphabetically. To open a collection, choose it from the Collections|Shared Library menu in the Library Browser.

  1. Community School of Davidson videos and experiments for middle school students: http://ejs.davidson.edu/tracker/CSD/CSD.xml
  2. Eckerd College Collection of video analysis and modeling experiments developed by Anne Cox: http://academics.eckerd.edu/physics/tracker/eckerd_tracker_collection.php
  3. High Point University Collection Tracker lab manual from Aaron Titus: https://physlets.org/tracker/HPU/HPU.xml
  4. LivePhoto Videos developed expressly for video analysis: http://livephoto.rit.edu/LPVideos/php_video_livephoto.xml
  5. NTNU Videos produced by Jonas Persson at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology: http://www.skolelab.ntnu.no/fysikk-animasjoner/Tracker/video/php_video_ntnu.php
  6. Singapore Tracker Digital Library from Loo Kang Wee and the Singapore Tracker community: http://iwant2study.org/lookangejss/indexTRZdl.php
  7. University of Cincinnati Collection of Tracker lab experiments from Kathleen M. Koenig and Sarah Baker: https://physlets.org/tracker/CINCY/CINCY.xml
